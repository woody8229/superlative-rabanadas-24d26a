Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PlateShift collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile applications and related services (the “Service”).

Plain-English promise: we do not sell your personal information.

Information we collect

  • Account information (e.g., email, name) if you create an account.
  • Recipe content you provide (e.g., recipe text, images you upload, imported links).
  • Usage data (e.g., feature usage, app performance) to improve reliability and UX.
  • Device information (e.g., OS version, device identifiers) for security and troubleshooting.

How we use information

  • Provide and operate the Service (saving recipes, generating grocery lists, shifts, etc.).
  • Improve features and fix bugs.
  • Prevent fraud and abuse.
  • Communicate with you (support requests, important notices).

AI features

If you use AI-powered features (like recipe shifting), we may send the text you provide (e.g., recipe ingredients/instructions and your selected preferences) to AI processing services to generate results. Do not include sensitive personal information in prompts or recipe notes.

Third-party services

We rely on third-party infrastructure to run PlateShift. Depending on how the app is configured, this may include:

  • Cloud database/auth/storage providers (e.g., Supabase)
  • Analytics providers (if enabled)
  • Advertising providers (Google AdMob, if ads are shown)

Data retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion as described below.

Your choices & rights

  • Access and update your profile in the app.
  • Request account deletion via Account deletion.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us via the Support page: support.
